The Creation Story

If you have found this page, then congrats! You're a nosy bastard who likes looking under the hood and kicking the tires in. I bet you've brought your own dipstick as well.

How did I craft this glorious monument to hubris, I hear you say? I'm glad you asked. Originally, I had heard of this nifty static site generator called Hugo . It was pretty cool! It was dead easy to get up and running, had a nice templating syntax. Beautiful. Easy. But most importantly, it worked! No faffing around with PHP or MySQL or any nonsense like that --- you could just upload the three files (or however many there were) to a server and, y'know, just serve them. I picked a theme I liked (of which you can definitely still see the remnants of), and I was away.

Flash-forward six months. I wanted to update something. Something tiny . Easy, surely? I made the change, and then ran Hugo. It absolutely pissed errors. So many errors. "Maybe there's an mistake in my template syntax?", I wondered naively. Alas, the only mistake I had made was using Hugo. There had been some major API change or something. I spent approximately 30 minutes trying to fix it before becoming frustrated and despondent.

Then it hit me - I'm a computer scientist! In theory , I can code. Why not just code it up by hand? So I set out, effectively doing a clean room reimplementation of the Hugo-generated site, and within a relatively short amount of time, I had a finished product. A beautiful, one-page website, my mug plastered across it, in pure HTML, CSS, Javascript.
