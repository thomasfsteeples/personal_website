About

I work for TWS Partners, a game theory consultancy. Prior to this, I completed my DPhil at the University of Oxford, working on quantitative rational verification. Broadly, my research interests lie across logic, complexity and games, and in particular, their application to Artificial Intelligence.

Outside of work, I enjoy cooking, trying to get an endless number of side projects off the ground, and playing chess poorly.
